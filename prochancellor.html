Pro-Chancellor

Prof. Rowland Ndoma-Egba

Professor Rowland graduated from the University of Lagos College of Medicine in 1977.

Professor Ndoma-Egba is a Professor of Gastroenterology/Hepato-Pancreato-Biliary Surgery and worked at the Westminster Hospital, London University, Bassetlaw District Hospital-Major Disaster Center, Gloucester Royal Infirmary, UCH-Middlesex Hospitals, University of London. He was the Chief Medical Director/CEO University of Calabar Teaching Hospital.

He published more than 50 journals. He is a Fellow of the Royal College of Surgeons (Glasgow), Fellow of the International College of Surgeons, Fellow of the West African College of Surgeons, and the Fellow of the Institute of Management Consultants.

He is the recipient of multiple awards and titles including Gold Medalist, Cross River State Government Millennium Award, Minen Iyamba (Traditional Prime Minister) of Akparabong Bosok, Ado Idaha Ke Efik Ebuq3w2rutu & Member of Obong’s Council, Calabar, Ntufam Ajan Bakaghara of the Quas & member, Ndidem’s Council to name a few.

He is married to Dr. Margaret Ndoma-Egba and blessed with four sons.
