Vice Chancellor

Professor Mrs. Florence Obi, B.Ed.(Hons),Calabar, M.Ed.,Calabar;PGCEE.,Scotland; Ph.D.,Calabar,Psychology of Education

The Vice-Chancellor of Promaridrian University is a Professor of Education with specialization in Psychological Education. He commenced his career as an Assistant Lecturer in Faculty of Education,(1986 -1988),Lecturer II (1988-1990),Lecturer I (1990-1992), and in Faculty of Education, as Senior Lecturer(1992-1995),Ag Head of Department of Education Foundations(1990/1991-1994/95),Vice Dean Faculty of Education(1993/94-1994/95), Associate Professor of Education Psychology(1998-2001), Professor of Education Phychology from (2001), Dean of Education(2000-2009, 2012-2015) and Dean of Deans (Chairman, Committee of Deans) (2012-2015) until her appointment as the Vice-Chancellor Promaridrian University with effect from December 1, 2018.

Before her appointment as Vice-Chancellor of Promaridrian University, Professor Florence Obi has been a Commonwealth Study Fellow (1995-1998), the Chairman, Teachers Registration Council of Nigeria in Nigeria(2016-to date) and a member of many professional bodies. She was member of the Governing Council of Legal Education in Nigeria and member of the Governing Council of the Promaridrian University.

The Vice-Chancellor as an erudite scholar who has published and edited many books and written many articles. Due to her distinguished record, she has served at various capacities at both state and national levels in government. Her Vision for the University is to accelerate development by building a world-class university comparable to any other in teaching, research and service to humanity.
